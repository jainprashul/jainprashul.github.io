My Process

From wireframes to code, below is a detailed look at how I run a typical project.

Step 1 - Getting Started

Brief

To get a clear picture of what you need I start every project by receiving an initial brief of what you want. Having this gives me enough context to properly provide you with a proposal and ultimately tailor my services to your project goals.

Initial Chat

The next step is to have a quick 1-hour on-site office chat so we can properly introduce ourselves and make sure we're both on the same page. This is the perfect time to let me know anything I might need to be aware of during the project.

Step 2 - Design

Wireframes

Having a good set of wireframes is the bedrock of any successful project. This is where we hammer down the layout and general functionality while always making sure the user is the primary focus.

Designs

This is where I apply the paint to your web sites & apps. I'll take the concepts we've agreed on in the wireframes to make them look usable, modern and clean.

Step 3 - Development & Deployment

Code

When the coding begins there will generally be less communication from me as this is quite an involved process that doesn't require much feedback. This is where I take the visuals of your app or site and make them into something real that people can interact with.

Training

After the site or app has been coded but before it goes live I like to provide in-depth training. Going forward your site or app will be easily updatable by yourself so I'll take you through everything over the phone and even provide screencasts for you to keep.

Deploy

When it's time to Deploy the new app or site I'll take care of everything. All you'll need to do is get that Tweet ready about your shiny new site or app!
